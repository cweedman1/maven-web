How to Write a Plan of Correction (POC)

A Plan of Correction (POC) is required when a facility receives deficiencies during a survey. It must clearly explain how the issue will be corrected, prevented, and monitored.

This guide explains how to write a Plan of Correction (POC) for nursing home survey deficiencies, including step-by-step examples.

Step 1: Identify the Deficiency

Clearly restate the deficiency. Be specific and ensure your response directly addresses the citation.

Step 2: Determine Root Cause

Identify why the issue occurred. Was it training, process failure, or system breakdown?

Step 3: Implement Corrective Action

Explain exactly what actions will be taken to correct the issue and who is responsible.

Step 4: Monitor and Prevent Recurrence

Describe how the facility will ensure the issue does not happen again.

Example Plan of Correction

Deficiency: Exit access not readily accessible.
Correction: Updated safety procedures and staff training implemented.
Monitoring: Weekly audits conducted by administration.
